Helping you understand your symptoms better

 



        Online doctor is an expert system which is use
	for simplifying the task of doctors.It is a system that
	checks a patient at initial level and suggests possible 
	diseases.On the basis of available cumulative information,
	the system will display the name and the prescribed medicine
	of the disease.It also provide the details of specialized
	doctor to talk with them.For registered people,message is 
	sent to remind them to take prescribed medicine.This system
	not only simplifies task of the doctors but also helps the 
	patients by providing initial medicines for small diseases
	in emergency.
