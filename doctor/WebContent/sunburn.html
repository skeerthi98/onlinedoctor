sun burn

Most people have had sunburn a reddened,irritated area of skin caused by overexposure to the sun's rays or other ultraviolet light. Sunburn can vary from mild to severe, depending partly on your skin type and how long you're in the sun.

Treatment

Treatment for sunburn may include:
  • Cool baths or compresses
  • Applying a light moisturizer
  • Prescription or over-the-counter pain relievers
  • Drinking liquids to replace body fluids or intravenous fluids
  • Staying out of the sun
  • Corticosteroid tablets
  • Oral antibiotics or antibiotic skin cream
  • Prescription sunburn cream
  • Hospital treatment
